about

planets are fixed gravity wells — each tied to a note. little moons drift between them pulled by gravity. the closer a moon gets to a planet, the louder that note sounds.

output is live — browser synth or midi (one channel per moon, CC#11 expression riding the volume in real time). start it and leave it on.

privacy

little moons runs entirely in your browser. your audio is never uploaded — all processing happens locally on your device. no data is collected.
